Lambda Dashboard

Getting started

To begin, click the "" button, located on either the left or right hand side of the screen, labelled "Dev Tools".

From there you can navigate around the solution: you're able to view all blocks, pages and layouts from the "Blocks" tab, all the associated states of your selection in the "Context" tab and edit the underlying JSON file for that state from the "Data" tab.
